About Me

I am currently completing a Level 4 Software Developer Apprenticeship at Geosoft Care. My responsibilities include web page development, bug fixes, application and website maintenance, and creating software solutions that improve efficiency for both staff and clients. This role has given me valuable experience working in a professional software development environment and has allowed me to apply and expand my technical knowledge in real-world projects.

Before starting my apprenticeship, I attended sixth form where I studied A-Level Computer Science, Mathematics and Business Studies. During this time, I was unsure whether to go to university or pursue an apprenticeship, but eventually chose to focus on starting my career through practical experience. Due to making this decision later in the application cycle, I was initially unable to secure a degree apprenticeship, as I had not completed enough extracurricular experience to strengthen my applications. Instead, I took a gap year to continue applying while also gaining experience and improving my skills. After several interviews, I was offered a Software Developer Apprenticeship at Geosoft Care in January 2026 and began my role shortly after.

My apprenticeship is delivered through BPP Education, alongside structured training courses in Software Development Fundamentals, Python Programming, Databases and Cyber Security. I have completed the Software Development Fundamentals module and am continuing to build my knowledge across the remaining areas. In my day-to-day work, I primarily use HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP to develop and improve features across web applications.

Looking ahead, my goal is to progress onto a Level 6 Degree Apprenticeship in Digital and Technology Solutions and continue building a long-term career in software engineering. I enjoy problem-solving and the process of turning requirements into working software, and I am particularly interested in expanding my knowledge of cyber security alongside development. To support this, I am currently completing learning modules on TryHackMe.

I am focused on continuing to improve my skills, gain real-world experience, and grow into a confident and capable software engineer over time.
